Introduction¶

In this project I will be working with No-Show-Appointments dataset which contains information about 100k medicak appointment inclusing information about the patients like thier Age, gender, wheather they received SMS or not,etc. And finally did they show for the appointment or not.

During the analysis I will try to answer the question: What factors are important for us to know in order to predict if a patient will not show up for their scheduled appointments?

Conclusions¶

Well I found that there are some relationships between some factors and people who didn't show:
1- people aged from 5 to 45 didn't show more
2-the less the days between the scheduled day and the appointment day the more the people don't show for the appointment
3- people who receive SMS show less for their appointments
4- People having Diabetes, Hipertension or Hadicap show more for their appointments
5- some factors show no strong relationship between them and showing up for the appointment like: Gender, Alcoholism

Limitations:
There are some limitation in the data like: it doesn't show the reason for the appointment, of course having an appointment before a surgery is way important and must go to rather than having an appointment after having a flu. Also it didn't show whether the person is a student, employed or unemployed.
