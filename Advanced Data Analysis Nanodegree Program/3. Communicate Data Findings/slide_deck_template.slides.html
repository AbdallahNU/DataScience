Effect of Players Characteristics on Their Values¶

Abdallah Mahmoud Elsayed¶

Investigation Overview¶

The main goal of this investigation was to find out what Characteristics could be used to predict player value. The main focus was on player Overall, Ball Control, International Reputation, and Body Type.

Dataset Overview¶

The dataset inlcude Innformation about more than 18K players from FIFA19 game, with all thier characteristics, and thier values.

Values Distribution¶

Players Values take on a very large range of values, from less than 10K ,𝑡𝑜 𝑎𝑏𝑜𝑢𝑡 102 million at the highest. Plotted on a logarithmic scale, the distribution of player values takes on a Normal shape.
